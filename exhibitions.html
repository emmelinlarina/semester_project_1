CURRENT EXHIBITIONS

Launchpad to the stars: The Engineering of Space Travel

Discover the Innovations and Mechanics behind Rocket Science.

We invite you to delve into the fascinating world of rocket science and space exploration. Discover the innovations and engineering marvels that propel us into the cosmos, from pioneering designs of early rockets to the cutting-edge technology shaping our journey to the stars

Explore the evolution of rocket engineering and witness the breakthroughs that have made space travel possible. Learn about the challenges and triumphs of designing rockets, from the early experiments to the sophisticated spacecraft of today.

Follow the trajectory of space exploration through time: from the fundamental principle of rocket propulsion to the ground breaking missions that have expanded our understanding of the universe. Gain insight into the key milestones and technological advancements that continue to drive our quest for space

Location:

Explorium Science Museum, Star hall

Robots Unleashed: The Evolution and impact of AI

Explore interactive displays that showcase the latest in robotic technology and AI applications.

We invite you to explore the fascinating world of robotics. The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here

You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot

Discover the journey of robotics from early mechanical automatons to the sophisticated, intelligent machines of today. Experience how AI is revolutionizing industries, enhancing daily life, and pushing the boundaries of what machines can achieve.

Location:
Explorium Science Museum, Main Hall

Life Uncovered: The Wonders of Biology

Experience the wonders of ecosystems and biodiversity.

We invite you to embark on a captivating journey through the diverse and intricate world of biology. This exhibition celebrates the complexity and beauty of living organisms, from the microscopic to the macroscopic, and explores the fundamental principles that govern life.

Dive into the fascinating realm of cellular biology and discover the building blocks of life. Explore interactive displays that illustrate the functions and structures of cells, tissues, and organs. See detailed models and simulations that bring this to life.

Location:
Explorium Science Museum, Main Hall

Energetica Exhibition on Loan

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum. It’s a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them. From solar energy powering lighting, to ‘Wind Island’ that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.

Location:
Explorium Science Museum, Main Hall
